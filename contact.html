TownCat

TownCat is an addictively cute endless runner arcade game with engaging Achievements and competitive Leaderboards.

Run and jump in this fun hardcore endless runner, that will challenge even the toughest TownCat!

Your goal is to jump to the top of your screen while avoiding obstacles and Get more score with Collect coins along the way.
